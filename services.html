High Performance Services

Structural

Structural engineering is the key to designing a new house or extending or altering an existing house. The structural engineer calculates loading to comply with Australian Standard.

Architecture

Architectural design provides a conceptual artwork of your dream home. Every internal and external element or component of the house is detailed to create a visually functional structure.

Insurance Claim

When damage or defect presents in your house, you want your insurance policy to cover the cost. We can help you produce the report that the insurance provider requires to support your claim.

Structural Design

We carry out residential house structural design, third-party design review and RPEQ sign-off. We have the software capacity to calculate the loading in a complaint with Australian Standard.

Extension, Renovation, Alteration

Structural inspection and calculation are needed if you want to add an extension or alter the layout of the existing house. QSC can help you conduct an analysis or calculation to ensure the support members in the extension or alteration are safe to be in place.

Pre-purchase Structural Assessment

Checking the structural integrity when purchasing an old house is always a good practice. Small defects in the structural system could lead to severe consequences. We help you investigate the entire structure of the house during your cooling-off period.

Site Analysis

Every site is different. Site conditions, e.g. climate, slope, wind, soil type, solar access, etc., are never the same. We will conduct an on-site strength and weakness analysis to determine the most suitable house type responding to your land.

Schematic Design

It is very common to ask for a house with unique or aesthetic features that you desire. Our architects will collect styles and needs from clients to create several concept house designs for the client to review and produce a final design upon incorporating feedback from the client’s review.

Construction Management

QSC helps you communicate with the local council to apply for house design approval, obtain quotes from builders, and coordinate the building process.

On-site Investigation

Insurance claims are evidence-based. We will conduct interviews with you and related persons and collect enough evidence on-site to compile the report to support your claim.

Building Pathology Analysis

Building pathology is an established theory and a systematic way of finding defects or potential risks in the building through investigation and analysis based on collected evidence. It is an effective way to find the root cause of the defect to better polish the report.

Structural Remediation Design

It is essential to rectify the damage or defect in the building as soon and as effective as possible. We help you produce the remediation plan and tailor the design to suit your budget and need.
